
# Business Insights Report

**Project:** IGP E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analysis
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and operational efficiency using transactional data.

* **Total Orders:** 1,000
* **Total Revenue:** ₹35.2 Lakhs
* **Average Customer Spend:** ₹3,520
* **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 days

The business demonstrates strong **seasonality, occasion-driven demand, and product concentration**, with clear opportunities in marketing optimization and operational efficiency.

---

## 2. Revenue Analysis

Revenue generation is **unevenly distributed**, with significant contributions from specific occasions and time periods.

### Key Observations:

* A small set of occasions and months contribute disproportionately to total revenue
* Revenue spikes indicate **event-driven purchasing behavior**

### Business Implication:

* Sales strategy should prioritize **high-impact periods and events**

---

## 3. Occasion-Based Performance

### Top Performing Occasions:

* Anniversary
* Raksha Bandhan
* Holi

### Underperforming Occasions:

* Diwali
* Valentine’s Day

### Insights:

* **Emotion-driven occasions** (relationships, gifting moments) outperform broader festivals
* Customer intent is stronger during **personalized celebration events**

### Recommendations:

* Increase targeted campaigns for high-performing occasions
* Re-evaluate marketing strategy and product offerings for underperforming events

---

## 4. Product Category Analysis

### High Revenue Categories:

* Colors
* Soft Toys
* Sweets

### Low Revenue Categories:

* Mugs
* Plants

### Insights:

* Products with **emotional or gifting appeal** significantly outperform utility-based items
* Revenue dependency is skewed toward select categories

### Recommendations:

* Promote high-performing categories with strategic visibility
* Bundle low-performing products with best-selling items
* Evaluate pricing or positioning of underperforming categories

---

## 5. Time-Based Order Behavior

### Peak Order Hours:

* Morning: 6 AM – 8 AM
* Evening: 6 PM – 8 PM

### Low Activity Period:

* Midday hours

### Insights:

* Orders are concentrated during **non-working or leisure hours**
* Customer engagement aligns with **daily routines**

### Recommendations:

* Schedule promotions and ads during peak hours
* Introduce time-based offers (flash sales) to maximize conversions

---

## 6. Monthly Sales Trends

### Peak Months:

* February
* August
* November

### Low Sales Period:

* April to July

### Insights:

* Sales are **highly seasonal and occasion-driven**
* Strong correlation between festivals/events and revenue spikes

### Recommendations:

* Align inventory and marketing campaigns with peak demand periods
* Introduce discounts or campaigns during low-demand months to stabilize revenue

---

## 7. Product Performance (Top SKUs)

* Top 5 products contribute a significant portion of revenue

### Insights:

* High reliance on a limited set of products
* Potential risk if demand shifts or supply issues occur

### Recommendations:

* Ensure consistent availability of top-performing products
* Identify and promote similar products to diversify revenue sources

---

## 8. Geographic Analysis

### Top Cities by Orders:

* Imphal
* Dhanbad
* Kavali

### Insights:

* Strong demand from **Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities**
* Market presence extends beyond metropolitan regions

### Recommendations:

* Strengthen logistics network in high-demand cities
* Implement localized marketing strategies

---

## 9. Delivery Performance

* **Average delivery time:** 5.53 days

### Insights:

* Delivery timelines are relatively high for e-commerce standards
* Potential impact on customer satisfaction and repeat purchases

### Recommendations:

* Optimize logistics and fulfillment processes
* Explore regional warehouses or faster shipping options

---

## 10. Strategic Recommendations

* Focus on **high-performing occasions and seasonal peaks**
* Optimize **product mix and category performance**
* Leverage **time-based customer behavior for marketing**
* Improve **delivery speed and operational efficiency**
* Expand presence in **high-demand non-metro regions**

---

## 11. Conclusion

The analysis highlights a business model driven by **occasion-based demand, seasonal trends, and key product categories**. By optimizing marketing strategies, improving logistics, and diversifying product offerings, the business can significantly enhance revenue growth and customer experience.
